Babylon 5

Why I Babylon 5

When writing about the TV series, it's easy to accidentaly spoil something for potential viewers, but as the series is very old by todays standards, perhaps this can be viewed as something to look forward to in the series, how they are presented?

What I love about Babylon 5 is the over-arching story that writer and producer J. Michael Straczynski created as well as the universe in wich the story exists in. The series was intended to be a five season show and that's exactly what it became. Therefore the ending was well thought-out and planned in advance as well as having an intended ending in the first place, something series that don't have a big picture idea lack.
